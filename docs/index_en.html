Privacy Policy – BalanceBook

Welcome to the BalanceBook app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and securing your personal information. Below are the details on how we collect, use, and store data:

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Account & Transaction Data: Includes amounts of income, expenses, and dates.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this data to improve user experience, provide technical support, and analyze usage in order to develop better financial features.

3. Data Storage & Protection

All data is stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database on your device. We do not send this data to external servers unless you explicitly opt in to cloud sync in a future version. Local encryption methods ensure that your data remains secure.

4. Permissions

  • INTERNET: For downloading updates and backups.
  • CAMERA: To scan barcodes only. No photos are captured or stored.
  • No other “dangerous” permissions are requested in this version.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent. If you choose to enable sync, your data may be uploaded to our secure cloud service (e.g., Firebase) to keep your information backed up and synchronized across devices, fully complying with industry-standard security practices.

6. Delete Account & Data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from within the app:

  1. Tap the “Delete Account” button in Settings. Upon confirmation, the app will:
    • Delete your authentication record (Firebase Auth).
    • Remove all transaction, invoice, and product data from Firestore.
    • Erase any files stored in Firebase Storage.
    • Delete the local SQLite database from your device.
    You will receive an in‑app confirmation once the process completes.

Note: This action is irreversible. Once confirmed, your account and data cannot be recovered.

7. User Rights

  • You have the right to view and selectively delete your data within the app.
  • Account deletion and data removal can only be performed via the in‑app “Delete Account” option.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last Updated” date will be noted at the bottom of this page.
