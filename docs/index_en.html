Privacy Policy – BalanceBook

Welcome to the BalanceBook app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and securing your personal information. Below are the details on how we collect, use, and store data:

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Account and Financial Transaction Data: Includes amounts of income, expenses, and dates.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this data to improve user experience, provide technical support, and analyze usage to develop better financial features.

3. Data Storage and Protection

All data is stored locally in an SQLite database on your device. We do not send this data to any external server unless you explicitly consent (e.g., subscribing to a cloud version in the future). Data inside the SQLite database is encrypted using local mechanisms.

4. Permissions

  • INTERNET: For downloading updates and backups in the future.
  • No other “dangerous” permissions are requested in this version.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your explicit permission. In case of logging out or automatic synchronization, your financial and personal data may be uploaded to external services such as Firebase to ensure its preservation and synchronization across your devices. We use these services fully complying with security and privacy standards to protect your data.

6. User Rights

  • You have the right to view all your data within the app and delete it (using the “Clear Data” button in app settings).
  • To request permanent deletion of all your data, please contact us via email: balancebook001@gmail.com.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last Updated” date will be noted at the top of this page.
